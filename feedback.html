How to Give Feedback on the Book...  

Via Email

Send comments and suggestions about the book or this website to feedback@producingoss.com; please use that address for translation inquiries too. And to make absolutely sure your mail gets through my spam filters, put the special marker [KNOTSPAM] in the subject line — that will guarantee that your mail is not eaten by my spam guards.

Please do not reproduce the above email address on web pages or in public postings.

I'm trying to reduce the likelihood of spam address harvesters learning about that email address; on this page it is encoded in various ways to fool the harvesters. To give someone an address for book feedback, just point them to this page's URL: http://producingoss.com/feedback.html.

The above email address goes straight to the author. If this book were a full-fledged open source project, there would be an open mailing list with web-accessible archives (among other things). However, the book stands somewhere between a single-author work and a collaborative project, and its supporting infrastructure is designed mainly to make things easy for the primary maintainer.
